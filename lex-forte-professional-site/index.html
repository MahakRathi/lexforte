The Approach

My methodology is rooted in a first-principles approach. I deconstruct complex legal challenges into their core components to build clear, robust, and effective solutions. Whether drafting a multi-clause agreement or analyzing a novel point of law, the focus remains on precision, strategic foresight, and delivering practical value. I believe the best legal work is not only technically sound but also serves as a clear instrument for achieving business and personal objectives.
